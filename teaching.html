Teaching

Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centers on creating an inclusive and engaging learning environment where students actively participate in their education. I believe in combining theoretical foundations with practical applications, encouraging critical thinking, and fostering intellectual curiosity.

I strive to make complex concepts accessible through real-world examples, interactive discussions, and hands-on problem-solving. My goal is to prepare students not just for exams, but for lifelong learning and professional success.
