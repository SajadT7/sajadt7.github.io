About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the University of California, Santa Cruz. My research interests lie in Applied Econometrics, Public Economics, and Energy Economics.

I use advanced econometric methods including causal inference, causal machine learning, and structural modeling to address important policy questions in immigration, education, and energy consumption.
