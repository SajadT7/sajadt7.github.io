Welcome!

I'm a third-year Ph.D. student in Economics at University of California, Santa Cruz.

My research lies at the intersection of Applied Econometrics, Public Economics, and Labor Economics, with a particular focus on immigration enforcement and local labor markets. I study issues related to immigration policy, crime, school choice, and demographic change.

I was also a Research Assistant at Sharif University of Technology. My work involved data analysis and structural modeling in the context of energy subsidy reform.
